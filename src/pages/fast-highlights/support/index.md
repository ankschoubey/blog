## Common Questions

### How do I highlight text?

Select any text on a webpage and use Fast Highlighter to instantly highlight it.

### How do I copy highlights?

Use the built-in shortcut or menu option to copy your saved highlights.

### Does Fast Highlighter sync data?

No. All data stays on your device for privacy.

### Why isn't it working in Safari?

Make sure the extension is enabled in Safari settings.
