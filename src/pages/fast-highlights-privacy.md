## Privacy Policy for Fast Highlighter

Fast Highlighter respects your privacy. This app is designed to work entirely on your device.

## No Data Collection

Fast Highlighter does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No analytics, tracking, or user profiling is performed.

## No Accounts Required

You do not need to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app.

## Local-Only Storage

All highlights and data remain on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers.

## No Third-Party Sharing

Since no data is collected, no data is shared with third parties.

## Permissions

The app only uses the permissions necessary to function within Safari. No unnecessary access is requested.
